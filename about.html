Who are we

We're a student-run pet store dedicated to making pet happiness accessible for all. Whether your furry friend needs toys, treats, or care supplies, we provide a variety of goodies to explore.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: provide pet supplies to everyone at affordable prices, making it easy for anyone to find their pet's next toy. We believe in the power of quality products to entertain, comfort, and delight pets.

Why Choose Us?

As a student project, we're passionate about building a community of pet people. We offer a collection of toys and treats for all kinds of pets, and our goal is to provide a welcoming spot for you and your best friend.
